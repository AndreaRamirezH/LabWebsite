We are a theoretical and computational lab focused on systems neuroscience. We are based in the Control Group at the University of Cambridge's Engineering Department and collaborate with experimentalists around the world. Our research interests include:

  • modelling of excitable cells and networks
  • online estimation of biophysical neural networks
  • representational drift and brain-machine interfaces
